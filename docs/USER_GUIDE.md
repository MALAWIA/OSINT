# NSE Intelligence Platform User Guide

## 🎯 Platform Overview

The NSE Intelligence Platform is a comprehensive tool for monitoring Nairobi Stock Exchange news, analyzing market sentiment, and participating in compliant financial discussions.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Registration

1. **Visit** [https://nseintelligence.com](https://nseintelligence.com)
2. **Click** "Sign Up" in the top right
3. **Fill** the registration form:
   - Username (unique, 3-20 characters)
   - Email address
   - Strong password
   - Display name (optional)
4. **Verify** your email address
5. **Complete** your profile (optional)

### Login

- Use your email and password
- Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)
- Choose "Remember me" for trusted devices

## 📰 News & Intelligence

### News Feed

**Features:**
- **Real-time Updates**: News appears within minutes of publication
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Color-coded sentiment indicators
- **Company Tagging**: Clickable company mentions
- **Source Attribution**: Always know the original source

**Understanding Sentiment:**
- 🟢 **Positive**: Good news, positive developments
- 🟡 **Neutral**: Factual reporting, no clear bias
- 🔴 **Negative**: Bad news, concerning developments

### Filtering News

**By Company:**
1. Click "Filter" in the news section
2. Select companies from your watchlist
3. Choose "Apply Filter"

**By Sentiment:**
1. Use sentiment filter buttons
2. Combine with company filters
3. Save filter preferences

**By Time:**
- Last hour
- Last 24 hours
- Last week
- Custom date range

### Company Profiles

**Access Methods:**
- Click company ticker in news (e.g., $SCOM)
- Search from top navigation
- Browse companies section

**Profile Information:**
- **Basic Info**: Ticker, sector, description
- **Market Data**: Market cap, listed date
- **Recent News**: Latest mentions and sentiment
- **Sentiment Timeline**: Historical sentiment trends
- **Event Detection**: Key events and announcements

## 💬 Discussion Channels

### Channel Types

1. **Stock Channels** ($SCOM, $EQTY, etc.)
   - Company-specific discussions
   - News-linked conversations
   - Technical analysis (informational only)

2. **General Channels**
   - `#general-market`: Overall market discussion
   - `#breaking-news`: Real-time news reactions
   - `#regulation-updates`: CMA and regulatory news

3. **Sector Channels**
   - `#banking`: Banking sector discussions
   - `#telecom`: Telecommunications sector
   - `#manufacturing`: Manufacturing companies

### Joining Channels

1. **Browse Channels**: Click "Channels" in navigation
2. **Join**: Click "Join" on any public channel
3. **Search**: Use search bar to find specific channels
4. **Create**: Request new channels (admin approval)

### Chat Etiquette

✅ **Do:**
- Share factual information
- Cite sources for claims
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Respect other users
- Report inappropriate content

❌ **Don't:**
- Provide investment advice
- Share insider information
- Post false rumors
- Use manipulative language
- Spam or flood channels

### Message Features

**Rich Formatting:**
- **Bold**: Use asterisks *important*
- **Italic**: Use underscores _emphasis_
- **Code**: Use backticks `data`
- **Links**: Automatically detected

**Reactions:**
- 👍 **Like**: Agreement or appreciation
- 👎 **Dislike**: Disagreement
- 🤔 **Thinking**: Thought-provoking
- 💡 **Insight**: Valuable contribution
- 📊 **Data**: Informational content

**Message Types:**
- **Regular**: Standard text messages
- **News-Linked**: Messages linked to news articles
- **System**: Automated notifications

## 🛡️ Safety & Security

### Account Security

**Password Security:**
- Minimum 12 characters
- Mix of letters, numbers, symbols
- Regular password changes
- No password reuse

**Two-Factor Authentication:**
- Enable 2FA for enhanced security
- Use authenticator app (recommended)
- Backup codes saved securely
- Recovery options configured

### Recognizing Scams

**Red Flags:**
- Guaranteed investment returns
- Insider information offers
- Requests for personal data
- Pressure to act quickly
- Unsolicited financial advice

**Report Suspicious Activity:**
1. Click "Report" on message/user
2. Select reason category
3. Add details (optional)
4. Submit for moderator review

### Privacy Protection

**Data Sharing:**
- Only share what you're comfortable with
- Review privacy settings regularly
- Understand data retention policies
- Exercise data deletion rights

**Safe Communication:**
- Don't share personal financial information
- Be cautious with external links
- Verify information before sharing
- Use platform messaging for initial contact
